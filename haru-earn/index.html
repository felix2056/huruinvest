    Frequently asked questions

    Can I withdraw my deposits before its ending date?
    A premature withdrawal during the lock-up period is not available. However, you may choose to withdraw your earnings from your Earn Plus lock-up.
    Can I make additional deposits to my Earn Plus or Earn Explore account during its lock-up period?
    You cannot make additional deposits to your Earn Plus or Earn Explore account during its lock-up period. If you wish to lock up more assets for more earnings, you can open up a new Earn Plus or Earn Explore account. There is no limit on the number of Earn Plus or Earn Explore accounts that you can open.
    How does HighLine Gold keep my precious metal assets secure?
    HighLine Gold is compliant with global AML / KYC standards and partnered with BitGo to protect your assets. In addition to this, to ensure the security of our client’s assets, we transfer assets deposited to the top rated third party depository in the world which have AAA rated insurance. We, nor the depository have had any security breach to date.
    How does Haru Invest manage my assets?
    Your assets are managed by our in-house precious metals team and global asset management partners that monitor the market status, volatility and strive to maximize returns. Your assets are allocated to risk-adverse strategies, such as trading based on Market Neutral Trading, arbitrage trading, market-making, and other proprietary strategy based on market inefficiency.
