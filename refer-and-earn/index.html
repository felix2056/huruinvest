    Frequently asked questions

    How can I get my unique referral code?
    You must meet the following requirements:
    (1) Your email address must be verified.
    (2) Your identity must be verified.
    Once youve met the requirements, you can log in to your account and view your referral code on your Refer and Earn dashboard.
    How can I get the Welcome Referral Reward?
    If youve been invited as a friend:
    - You are eligible to receive the Welcome Referral Reward of $ in BTC if you are a new Haru Invest member or have an existing account with an applied referral code and have never created a lockup. Once youve signed up, you must lock up at least $ to get this reward.
    If youre a host:
    - You are eligible to get the Welcome Referral Reward of $ in BTC if your friend locks up at least $.
    Important Notice:
    • Please note the Welcome Referral Reward offer is no longer available at this time.
    How many referral rewards can I get?
    If youre a host, there is no limit to the number of referral rewards you can get.
    - You can get the $ BTC Welcome Referral Reward every time you invite a friend who meets the lockup requirements.
    - If you invite friends or more, you are eligible to get the Earn Referral Reward which is 2 to 5% of your friends earnings from Earn Plus and Earn Explore.
    If youre an invited friend, you can only receive the Welcome Referral Reward once.
    What are the different referral levels?
    Level 1: All hosts with unique referral codes
    Level 2: Hosts who have invited friends or more and have received the Welcome Referral Reward.
    - Friends who close their accounts after meeting the lockup requirement still count towards your minimum friends.
    Level 3: Hosts who have reached level 2, expressed interest in becoming brand ambassadors, and have been officially approved to be Haru Invest ambassadors.
