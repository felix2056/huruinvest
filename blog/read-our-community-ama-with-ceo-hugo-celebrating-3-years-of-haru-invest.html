Read our community AMA with CEO Hugo: Celebrating 3 years of Haru Invest

September 11, 2022September 7, 2022 by haruinvest

On Sunday, September 4, Hugo answered r/HaruInvest members’ questions and concerns on Haru Invest.

It’s a festive season for Haru Invest. We are celebrating 3 years of Haru Invest and in case you have missed it, we have obtained an MSB license from U.S. FinCEN, and have raised new funding at a $284M valuation.

To thank our community members, our CEO Hugo held an AMA to answer questions about our recent addition of XRP, continued high earn rates and addressed current market conditions: your trust matters.

Key takeaway

  • On Sunday, September 4, Hugo answered r/HaruInvest members’ questions and concerns on Haru Invest
  • Celebrating our 3rd anniversary and our recent updates, Hugo and the team was more than happy to talk to our community members — Talk to you again soon!

Ask Hugo Anything: Celebrating our 3rd anniversary Highlights

Due to a large volume of questions, Hugo was not able to cover all of the questions that were asked but they were all noticed. If you didn’t get a reply, don’t worry. Hugo took note of it.

Table of Contents

  • Our high earn rates
  • Withdrawals
  • XRP
  • Security, future updates and suggestions

Our high earn rates

Q. How does Haru Invest actually sustain these high returns? What are the risks to this business model?

Hugo: We are not only making profit from arbitraging but utilizing diverse inefficiency between crypto derivative market.

From the perspective of arbitrage, since 2019 when we first started, we knew that the arbitraging between spot exchange will become less profitable, from then we changed to inefficiencies in derivatives/futures.

Now we are focused on gaps and differences in basis of future market and funding fees.

Other than arbitraging, we’re also developing strategies in the option trading and stat arb field.

So we’re not exposed to Korean market premium. Hope this answers your questions.

Q. How does Earn Explore works to realistically generate 25% APR? What risks are clients’ BTC subject to and how you mitigate these risks?

Hugo: Our Earn Explore’s 25% APR is a target earn rate and we try our best to meet those earn rates. And we bring you between 15-20%.

Our strategy is not exposed to the price of BTC/ETH — rather we focus on increasing the amount of BTC/ETH.

Q. Honestly, do you think historic Haru Explore rates will remain sustainable as more users flock to the system?

Hugo: These days I’ve been realizing that the crypto market inefficiency is much bigger than I used to think. I don’t see a big drop in the near future.

Withdrawals

Q. Currently the withdrawal times out of Haru Wallet are slow. Does Haru have any plans to start keeping sufficient funds in a hot wallet?

Hugo: I know users are experiencing inconveniences with slow withdrawal times. Despite of those issues, most of our crypto asset is being traded continuously in the crypto derivatives market under our strategy — so in order to bring you the best earn rate, your crypto asset has to be kept being traded — not kept in a hot wallet.

Also, hot wallets can easily be exposed to hacks. Therefore, I do not think it is safe to support instant withdrawals via hot wallets.

XRP

Q. Lots of people have been critical of your recent addition of XRP to the platform. What would you say to them to assuage their concerns?

Hugo: We added XRP to reduce your withdrawal fees from the other crypto exchanges.

The SEC’s recent developments regarding the charges on Ripple indicates that it is unlikely held that Ripple is deemed as a registered security.

Of course, this is a speculation by some legal experts and others might have different views but XRP is anyway not being traded in the U.S major exchanges at the moment and even if the case is finally adjudicated in favor of SEC, its impact would be limited to the U.S jurisdictions.

Given that the status quo remains unchanged whether settled or proceeding to a court trial,, we didn’t see the recent take down on Ripple by SEC as a critical risk. The most important reason behind adding XRP was enabling users to add crypto to Haru Invest with low fees. Hope this answers your concerns.

Security, future updates and suggestions

Q. Security is a big concern for most users. Withdrawal address whitelisting would be a good step. Any plans for security upgrades?

Hugo: I am very well aware of security issues. We are reviewing address whitelisting. I will keep you posted on future security upgrades.

Q. In terms of the ETH move to POS and the likely fork / creation of ETHW, what are Haru Invest’s plans to deal with this?

Hugo: There has been mention of a possible new token (ETHW), which would be hard forked from Ethereum. The issuance of such a token however, has not been made clear yet and we will be sure to closely monitor any updates and provide you with additional information.Haru Invest will temporarily pause all ETH and USDT (ERC-20 series tokens) deposits and withdrawals due to ERC-20 network issues that may occur before or after the Ethereum Merge. This will begin 24 hours before the Merge and until we provide you with further notice. After confirming the stability of the ERC-20 network, we will allow all deposits and withdrawals. Please note this timeline may be subject to change.

Q. Will Haru Invest consider support for polygon or BSC networks to facilitate cheaper deposits and withdrawals for all tokens/coins?

Hugo: I acknowledge that it is important for users to use our service with low fees — that’s why we have added XRP — for reduced in/out fees. In the future, we will consider adding BSC/Polygon networks.

Q. How long will it be until USDC is supported on Haru Invest? Also, given the lower volume and more limited trade pairings for USDC compared with USDT. Can Hugo give a rough estimate of where he sees yield on USDC products being?

Hugo: USDC – I’m working on it. Compared to USDT, yes there is less opportunities for USDC but we will try our best to bring you the best earn rates. You’ll be able to know the exact earn rates when we actually integrate it to our service.

Q. Will you make it possible to add more funds to existing Earn Plus deposits?

Hugo: Our earn rate changes every two weeks so it will be realistically difficult to add more funds to existing Earn Plus deposits.

Q. For marketplace, is there any chance that Haru Invest could allow buyers to submit offers for frosts?

Hugo: Freeze Markteplace is considering various options to help users’ experiences and bidding(or giving offer for this case) is of the options we are considering.

As Marketplace grows and shows steady needs Haru be more than happy to provide with enough tools and functions for the users.
