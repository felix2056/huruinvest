1) Lock-up Promotion: Earn up to 3% more

To earn more, lock up your crypto for 90 days or more with Haru Earn Plus.

Earn rates may change depending on lock-up period

  • Over 90 days: +1%
  • Over 180 days: +2%
  • 365 days: +3%

For example, a 365 days lock-up = 14.2% base earn rate + 3% additional earn rate = Total of 17.2% earn rate APR

  • Over 90 days: +1% → Max earn rate 11.7% (in USDT)
  • Over 180 days: +2% → Max earn rate 13.8% (in USDT)
  • 365 days: +3%
    • USDT 14.2% → 17.2%
    • BTC 13.5% → 16.5%
    • ETH 13% → 16%
