Haru Invest acquires Money Service Business (MSB) License from U.S. FinCEN

September 11, 2022August 24, 2022 by haruinvest

As an official MSB across all 50 U.S. states, Haru Invest is now approved to carry out operations as a money transmitter service

Main takeaway

  • Haruus LLC, a U.S.incorporated subsidiary for Haru Invest has acquired a Money Service Business(MSB) license from U.S. FinCEN
  • As an official MSB across all 50 U.S. states, Haru Invest is now approved to carry out operations as a money transmitter service

Haru Invest members, we are thrilled to announce that Haruus LLC, a U.S.incorporated subsidiary for Haru Invest has acquired a Money Service Business (MSB) license, a financial service license supervised and granted by the U.S. Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (U.S. FinCEN) mandatory for any digital asset-related service providers to conduct businesses in compliance with U.S. regulations.

“Obtaining our MSB license in the U.S. is another key step in our top-line mission to provide stable, safe and compliant crypto investment opportunities for everyone, everywhere.” – Hugo Lee, CEO of Haru Invest.

Table of Contents

  • Why is MSB license important?
  • What will happen now?
  • About Haru Invest

Why is MSB license important?

Haru Invest’s MSB license follows the global asset management platform’s ongoing process of obtaining applicable licenses in global jurisdictions, creating a foundation of integrity, trust and compliance with customers and regulatory agencies across the world.

“Doing business within the ambit of this regulatory framework within the massive U.S. market will afford more transparency and protections for not only our clients, also our business itself.” – Hugo Lee, CEO of Haru Invest.

What will happen now?

As an official MSB across all 50 U.S. states, Haru Invest is now approved to carry out operations as a money transmitter service — including digital assets such as cryptocurrencies — and expand its scope of service in the country.

About Haru Invest

Haru Invest is a trusted digital asset management platform offering crypto deposit services and crypto investment products since 2019. We generate and distribute stable earnings through high frequency algorithmic trading, unlike other CeFi platforms that are based on lending/borrowing models. We have over $2 billion in total transaction volume with users from over 140 countries and have paid out earnings over 4.1 million times with zero security breaches.
