(Notice) Referral Program: as of Oct 16, 2022

November 22, 2022October 16, 2022 by haruinvest

We would like to let you know about some important referral program notifications.

Thank you for your enthusiastic interest in Haru Invest’s new referral program, which launched on October 11. Due to each region’s budget, we will temporarily suspend Ukraine’s referral program as of 18:00 (UTC) on October 16.

The welcome referral reward is not paid for friends who signed up after the suspension. We will deliver no rewards to the host either. Also, you can no longer find your referral code in your Haru mobile application. 

However, you can still use all services of Haru Invest, including deposits and withdrawals, and we will not ask you to return the rewards already paid to you. We sincerely appreciate your interest in Haru Invest in Ukraine, and we will come back to you shortly with a new referral program. 

If you have any questions, please get in touch with our Help Center.
