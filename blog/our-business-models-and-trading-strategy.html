Our Business Models and Trading Strategy

November 17, 2022November 17, 2022 by haruinvest

We have always built on sustainable models. This includes our business models and operations. Three years later, Haru Invest continues to develop new strategies and operations, including developing a market-neutral strategy by increasing the amount of cryptocurrency regardless of price volatility, to ensure its safety and security.

Built atop sustainable models

Dating back to our first days in 2019, the asset management team at Haru Invest has believed that cryptocurrency will become an incredibly valuable and widely used asset, and as such its management will be done by with the same passion, care and drive for strong returns as in conventional financial markets.

From the beginning, we have been built on top of sustainable models. That includes our operation strategies and business models. And to this day three years later, we continue striving to develop new strategies and operations — including developing a market-neutral strategy by increasing the amount of cryptocurrency regardless of its price volatility — that make Haru Invest the safest and most secure platform possible.

And despite the tumultuous situation and crypto winter this industry finds itself in, we have successfully proved that our strategies work to help manage and grow our users’ crypto assets every day regardless of market conditions.

Haru Invest and algorithmic trading

Since its launch in 2019, the Haru Invest trading strategy has been guided by algorithmic trading, not borrowing and lending.

We deploy three core algorithmic trading strategies to drive profits for investors:

  1. Arbitrage trading that leverages the price gap between crypto exchanges
  2. Market neutral strategy based on the price stability mechanisms at crypto futures exchanges
  3. Spread trading that focuses on the volatility of BTC/ETH futures contracts

We find inefficiencies between different crypto futures markets that did not previously exist in the traditional financial market. And by using this mechanism in the crypto futures market, Haru Invest can generate profits by using a hedging strategy that leverages a futures contracts mechanism to hedge a spot market. In other words, this strategy is using perpetual futures’ funding fees to generate profits by hedging the risks of future contracts in the spot market.

To this end, we have continuously developed a market-neutral strategy by increasing the amount of cryptocurrency regardless of its price volatility.

Find out more about our investment strategies here: https://haruinvest.com/blog/haru-invest-asset-management-strategy/
