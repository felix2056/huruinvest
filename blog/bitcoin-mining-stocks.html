Yet, solo crypto mining is no longer a promising road toward crypto mining. Nowadays, mining cryptocurrency collectively proves to be more profitable. 

Crypto mining has the necessary hardware and employs a large pool of miners. As a result, they have a higher mining capacity to generate a considerable income.

What Are Crypto Mining Stocks?

To understand crypto mining stocks, let’s first differentiate them from crypto mining. Crypto mining and mining of precious metals are very alike. However, instead of gold and silver, crypto miners work on crypto coins.

Crypto miners validate the transactions of a cryptocurrency’s blockchain network. They do so by solving complex mathematical equations called cryptographic hashes. This ensures that a digital currency isn’t spent twice. 

Miners compete to solve the hash value of a crypto coin transaction. The first crypto miner to crack the code and add the block to the ledger will receive cryptocurrency as an incentive for their work in the transaction validation process.

Meanwhile, the shares of crypto mining companies are what we call crypto mining stocks. Two common crypto mining stocks include Ethereum and Bitcoin mining stocks.

Bitcoin Mining Stocks

Experts advise those who want to invest in crypto without buying crypto itself to buy stocks. Specifically, the stocks of a company that has a financial stake in cryptocurrency. 

Take, for instance, Bitcoin mining companies. Such mining companies will work to mine as much Bitcoin to increase their profit. Hence, the mining company will use all its working capital towards the mining of Bitcoin.

When the company succeeds in doing so, it’ll have higher chances of mining bitcoins earlier than its competitors. This also means they can mine more Bitcoins within a day, increasing the company’s value.

Best Crypto Mining Stocks to Invest In

Various crypto mining groups are emerging as news of high crypto mining profitability spreads all over the world. Among the array of choices, we’ve compiled a list of the most promising crypto mining stocks for you.

Bitfarms

Bitfarms is an infrastructure company that provides computing power to crypto networks. This self-mining crypto company mainly validates Bitcoin blockchain transactions.

Its push for the development of its infrastructure helped increase its production limit. To date, it’s approaching its goal of producing three exahash per second.

The increase in Bitfarms’ hash rate made it possible for the company to produce 12.5 Bitcoins per day. Hence, the company prevails as a promising investment considering its mining capacity.

Hut 8 Mining

Hut 8 Mining is another crypto miner focused on the Bitcoin blockchain. The company relies on its multiple mining facilities and access to 109-Megawatt power. This massive power enables the crypto mining company to meet its mining needs. 

The company further boasts a variety of mining infrastructures. Such are well-adept at espousing the different volatility levels of the market. Not to mention its hash rate capacity of 4.5 exahash per second.

Marathon Digital

Marathon Digital is another capable Bitcoin-centered crypto mining company. Its currently working to increase the computing power of its Bitcoin miners. 

It’s acquiring more supercomputing machines to increase its hash rate. The company also has more than 32,000 crypto miners increasing their production. Further, Marathon Digital secured its power source by partnering with energy suppliers.

This strong commitment of Marathon Digital towards its development benefits its shareholders. The company now has a market cap of $2.3 billion. Stock shares of the company start at $22.

Canaan Inc

As a leading provider of supercomputing solutions, Canaan is one of the best crypto mining stocks to hold. Canaan catalyzed the Bitcoin mining sector by inventing the first Bitcoin mining machine.

Since then, the company has continued to provide state-of-the-art solutions. From energy-efficient computing equipment to the world’s first commercial edge AI chip.

Truly, acquiring Canaan Inc stocks promises nothing but an auspicious profit. In 2021 alone, it raked a blazing $447.3 million in revenue.

HIVE Blockchain Technologies Ltd

If you want a little variety, stocks of HIVE make a good option. Unlike most of the crypto companies here, aside from Bitcoin, HIVE mines Ethereum. This gives the company a wider range of sources of income.

The company is also expanding its cloud mining capacity, increasing its profit margin. It also uses green energy sources, making it an environment-friendly investment option. 

Riot Blockchain Inc

Rot Blockchain stayed true to its promise of attaining a hash rate capacity of 12.6 exahash per second. From increasing their power capacity to upgrading their crypto mining equipment. The company has done nothing but grow and improve its financial statement.

Its total revenue, for instance, jumped from $12.1 million in 2020 to $213.2 million in 2021. Moreover, its net loss also decreased to $7.9 million from $12.1 million in 2020. Such improvement is enough to prove that their shares are a worthy investment.
