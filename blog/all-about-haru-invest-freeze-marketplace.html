All About Haru Invest Freeze Marketplace

January 2, 2023 by haruinvest

With our new Freeze, you can easily sell and purchase your own Earn Plus products. Here are some tips for making a smart investment decision.

The start of Freeze Marketplace: Our members asked, Haru Invest answered.

“I have an urgent issue. I’d like to cancel my Earn Plus lockup.”

“I really love my Earn Plus, but can you add an option to cancel before the maturity date?”

There has been a lot of request for canceling an Earn Plus lockup. However, our asset management strategy wasn’t able to provide cancellation before the maturity date. At Haru Invest, we manage your assets based on the period of your Earn Plus lockup and offer high earn rates and secure stability.

Since our members have been asking for canceling an Earn Plus lockup, we put our heads together. Finally, you can trade your Earn Plus lockups with other Haru Invest members and fully liquify your assets.

Freeze Marketplace: Three things you should know

Freeze Marketplace is a place for our members to trade their Earn Plus lockups. Here are the main three things you should know about our Marketplace.

1. Purchase an Earn Plus lockup

  • Buy an Earn Plus lockup with a slightly higher earn rate at a discounted price.

2. Sell an Earn Plus lockup

  • Provide liquidity for an Earn Plus lockup.
  • Easily own a long-term period Earn Plus lockup.

3. Earn Plus and Frost

  • On the Marketplace, an Earn Plus lockup is turned into a Frost and can be traded.
  • Freeze an Earn Plus lockup to make it into a Frost. When it’s turned into a Frost, the principal amount, leftover earnings, and the total lockup period gets fixed.
  • Melt your Frost to turn it into an Earn Plus lockup.
  • When an Earn Plus is turned into a Frost, Haru Wallet earnings are paid out.

Start using Freeze: How to use the Freeze Marketplace

Trade your Earn Plus lockups on the Freeze Marketplace with other Haru Invest members. Here is an easy guide on many features of Freeze and how you can buy and sell Earn Plus lockups. Read on for a tip for purchasing Earn Plus lockups.

Getting to know Freeze Marketplace

1. Turn an Earn Plus lockup into a Frost and sell it

  • Freeze an Earn Plus lockup to get one Frost.
  • On the Freeze Marketplace, you can turn an Earn Plus lockup into a Frost and trade it.
  • Different Frosts can be created based on the Earn Plus lockup. A Frost contains the principal amount, lockup period, earnings payouts, and earn rates with a digital art NFT.
  • However, when it’s a Frost, earnings are paid out daily based on the most recent Haru Wallet earn rates.
  • When an Earn Plus lockup is turned into a Frost, the lockup period will not decrease. Melt the Frost to turn it back into an Earn Plus lockup.

2. Collect Frost and turn it into a Crystal.

  • Crystal is a broader concept of a Frost and can be created by collecting specific Frosts.
  • Enjoy special benefits from a Crystal. Earn a Crystal to boost your Earn Plus lockup(s) earn rates while you own it and enjoy a discount on your Earn Explore performance fees.
  • Crystal holders can earn daily payouts in their Haru Wallet. The most recent earn rates for the Haru Wallet are applied.
  • Crystal is a seasonal item and Season 1 has been sold out. Stay on the lookout for Season 2!

3. Making everything possible through Freeze and Melt

  • Freeze and Melt helps changing the form of Earn Plus and Frost.
  • Freeze your Earn Plus lockup and turn it into a Frost to sell it. Melt the Frost to turn it back to an Earn Plus lockup.
  • After a Frost or a Crystal is ‘Melt’, digital art and the benefits that are tagged to it will no longer exist.
  • Melt a Frost anytime you’d like.
  • Crystal and Frost that is used for a Crystal can Melt after 30 days after it is first created.

4. Let’s trade away on the Freeze Marketplace!

  • On Freeze Marketplace, anyone can trade Frost—an Earn Plus lockup after it goes through ‘Freeze’.
  • Liquidity for Earn Plus has increased because now you can sell an Earn Plus lockup before its maturity date.
  • You can easily signup for a long term, high earn rate Earn Plus lockup without having to start from day 1. Also sell your Earn Plus lockup with ease to fit your financial needs.
  • Buy an Earn Plus lockup with a slightly higher earn rate at a discounted price.

Your guide to trading Earn Plus lockups on the Marketplace

Here’s how you can purchase and sell Earn Plus lockups on the Marketplace in easy steps.

1. Selling an Earn Plus lockup.

Step 1 – On the Freeze tab, view your Earn Plus lockups.

(If you don’t have any Earn Plus lockups, you can proceed to create an Earn Plus lockup.)

Take a look at your Earn Plus lockups and choose the one you’d like to sell.

Step 2 –  Now it’s time to turn your Earn Plus lockup into a Frost—you must Freeze your Earn Plus lockup and turn it into a Frost in order to sell it on the Freeze Marketplace.

Step 3 – Register your Frost on the Marketplace.

Step 4 – Click on the ‘List it now’ button to register it on the Marketplace. You can change the price of your Frost at anytime by clicking the tabs in the Frost detail page.

Step 5 – After the Frost is sold on the Marketplace, the amount will be deposited into your Haru Wallet. Go to your Haru Wallet and see the ‘Recent Transactions’ page to take a look at the exact amount.

2. Buy a Frost

Step 1 – Take a look around in the Marketplace and purchase a Frost that fits your needs. Click on each Frost to read details such as discount rate, final earn rates.

Step 2 – After you have found a Frost that you would like to purchase, click on ‘Buy Now’.

(Some Frost requires a certain cryptocurrency to purchase it. If you don’t have enough crypto, you can use Switch to swap between different cryptocurrencies.)

Step 3 – Melt your Frost to turn it back into an Earn Plus lockup.

By purchasing an Earn Plus lockup as a Frost, you can enjoy relatively better earn rates at a discounted price.
