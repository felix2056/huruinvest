New Year, New Us: Welcome to HaruBank 2.0.

December 7, 2022December 24, 2019 by haruinvest

Happy holidays, everyone! New year is always full of joy, positivity, and ambitious plans. With new HaruBank 2.0, your new year is about to get more jolly. In this post, we would like you to meet our new website and a brand logo. It’s new year, new us!

Introducing Our New Brand Identity

With this renewal, we hope to convey more consistent, clear message to our clients. (And of course, also to become more sleek.) We put our heart into designing a new logo which will send out a clear message and our philosophy. And we are happy to share with you what the process was like! Here are three design principles that have guided us through.

The three most important values that we would like to deliver to our clients are: Trustworthy, Instant, Maximize (Your Profit on Your Crypto). Hence, we decided to come up with a logo that is a) visually stable, b) dynamic, and c) extendable. Combining the letter H, letter B, and the shape of a safe — and ta da! A new brand symbol of HaruBank was made.

A New Look of Our Website

Major changes have been made to our website, too. On our new website, we introduce who we are, what we offer, and why we do what we do in a more coherent manner. Most of our users already found that all the details of our offers on our website easy to read and understand. You can check it out with this link.

We added some of the testimonials from our clients — including Lihan who works as CEO/CTO at Xangle, Czhang, CEO at Piexgo, and Sieun, CEO at Nonce. We look forward to adding more testimonials and messages from our clients as our community grows.

Stay Tuned for HaruBank 2.0

New brand identity and website are just a mere beginning of our plan. HaruBank is planning to offer our clients better services and better reasons to stay tuned for us. If you have any questions about our new website or our services, please visit our website and leave a message down below. Or feel free to shoot us any questions at support@harubank.com.

About HaruBank

HaruBank is the trusted crypto savings and deposits service provider. We offer over 10% interest rate on cryptocurrency savings and up to 15% for monthly deposits of BTC, ETH, USDT, and Terra KRT. What we offer aims to shift a paradigm of investment in cryptocurrency — investment in crypto can be stable and comfortable, too. Keep your crypto asset smart.
